Discover Your Skin Type

Understanding your skin type is the first step towards choosing the best skincare products.

Normal Skin

Balanced sebum production and good blood circulation make normal skin neither too oily nor too dry.

Oily Skin

Oily skin has a greasy appearance due to excess sebum production, making it prone to acne breakouts.

Dry Skin

Characterized by a flaky and rough texture, dry skin can feel tight and sometimes cause irritation.

Combination Skin

A mix of oily and dry skin, usually with an oily T-zone and dry cheeks.

Sensitive Skin

If your skin reacts to spicy food and new products, you may have sensitive skin.
